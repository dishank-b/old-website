Research Internship at The Hi-Tech Robotic Systemz Ltd.

|
Hazy Image, Ground Truth, Output
Award in Recognition of Outstanding Contribution

Here is the the complete report of the work.

I completed my research intership during May to July 2018 at The Hi-Tech Robotic Systemz Ltd. (THRSL) under the supervision of Mr. Gaurav Singh who is Techincal Manager of their ADAS team, also he is alumnus of Carnegie Mellon University. THRSL is premier company in field of autonomous vehicles and ADAS in India. I worked on Real Time Image Dehazing for ADAS Using Deep Learning I had to develop a algorithm which can make their ADAS product work in extreme weather conditions such as fog, haze, rain, etc. Hence, I made a deep learning based model which makes their ADAS product haze/fog robust. After extensive literature review and experimentation I designed a GAN based deep learning network which takes a hazy/foggy image and outputs the corresponding clear image. The challenge was to develop a real-time algorithm for the ADAS product which had low end hardware. We all know, how much deep learning models can be computationally expensive. Hence, I did exhaustive study for deep learning optimiztion techniques and architecture and successfully built a network which works at more than 200 FPS on Nvidia 1080Ti and around 20 FPS on Nvidia Jetson Tx1.
